TGCal Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

TGCal helps users import roster PDFs and add flights to Apple Calendar.

Data Handling

  • Roster PDF parsing is performed on-device in the app.
  • TGCal does not require users to create an account.
  • TGCal does not sell personal data.

Calendar Access

TGCal requests Calendar permission only to create events the user explicitly chooses to add.

Third Parties

TGCal does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact tgcal.app@gmail.com.
